The winners are those 'that played football': Spanish fans celebrate World Cup win
EUROPE
Issued on: 20/07/2026 - 11:03
Share
Spain won the World Cup final 1-0 against Argentina. The final was deadlocked until Spain's Ferran Torres scored in extra time. It was a tough night for Argentina's Lionel Messi, regularly described as the best player of all-time, who was unable to score in what is likely to be his last World Cup final, paving the way for France's Kylian Mbappé towards the Golden Boot.
