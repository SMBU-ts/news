'Absolute joy spilling out' for Spain's 'new golden generation' of football
EUROPE
Issued on: 20/07/2026 - 11:03
Share
By winning the World Cup final against Argentina, Spain has become the first country to hold the men's and the women's title at the same time. The men's team is now at 38 consecutive successful games, a record. Now, Madrid is preparing for the celebrations that will mark the return of La Roja to its home. FRANCE 24's Sarah Morris tells us more on the atmosphere in Madrid.
